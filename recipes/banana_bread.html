Mom's Banana Bread

Description

Mom's homemade recipe for banana bread that we want to try.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup of flour (almond, hemp, coconut. Grind to flour if needed)
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • 5 dates, chopped
  • 3 bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • Dash of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 cup of olive oil
  • Dash of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients first
  2. Mash up banana into a mix
  3. Mix all wet ingredients, then all ingredients together
  4. Blend all ingredients for a few minutes
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes
